05

代码演化从 first-feasible 转向 guarded best-delta

L-layer evidence: evolution changes algorithmic structure, not only objective values

Gen 1

first feasible

找到第一个可行 Assign 后立即提交；简单 fallback。

Gen 3-4

trial-all + delta

遍历多个 Assign，计算 cost delta，并显式 rollback。

Gen 5-8

best-delta + fallback

选择最小 cost increase；失败时创建/使用新 Assign。

d75 Gen 6+

weighted best-delta

加入 slack / penalty / normalized delta，适配更密集实例。

Note: per-generation pops_best objectives are internal EOH selection signals; final claims use guarded external evaluations.
